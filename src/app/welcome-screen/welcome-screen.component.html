Journal Entries

We packed journals with tons of useful features for you to enjoy!

Journal entries made in Mirror Journal are mostly text based so we created a distraction-free editor that makes it easy to style your text.

Journal entries can also be tagged with fun emoji reactions that can show context around your day. You can also add an optional image header to really spice up your entry.

You can lock/encrypt their journals with a click of a button and also set expiration dates to have your journal entry deleted after some time!
